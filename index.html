Biography

Ioannis Galbos / Stockholm, Sweden

Now based in Stockholm, his musical journey began circa 2005 in Skopje, where he started crafting electronic music for the dancefloor. Tracks released by LAUS MUSIC (London), Juan's Kitchen Delights (Skopje), and 2 Rhythm 4 Record (UK).

He has released several EPs on Juan’s Kitchen Delights Recordings. Some of his favorite artists include Lawrence (Sten) and Tim Paris, a fact that shows the many shades to be found in his work.

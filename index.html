Ioannis Galbos

Bio

Ioannis Galbos / Stockholm, Sweden

Tracks released by LAUS MUSIC - London, Juan's Kitchen Delights - Skopje and 2 Rhythm 4 Record - UK.

Now based in Stockholm, his musical journey began circa 2005 in Skopje, where he started crafting electronic music for the dancefloor. He has released several EPs on Juan’s Kitchen Delights Recordings, and has also appeared on LAUS Records and 2 Rhythm 4 Record. Some of his favorite artists include Lawrence (Sten) and Tim Paris, a fact that shows the many shades to be found in his work.
